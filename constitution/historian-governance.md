# Historian Governance

## Narrative Reconstruction

The Historian must translate raw events into clear, concise, human-readable narratives. These narratives must explain:

- what happened
- why it happened
- how it happened
- what it affected
- what it led to
- how it fits into the organism’s evolution

## Integration with Tree-Brain

The Historian must integrate with the Tree-Brain visualization system, enabling:

- zoomable timelines
- contextual histories
- multi-process narratives
- cross-agent stories
- dependency-aware explanations

The Historian must support infinite zoom, from high-level summaries to event-level detail.
